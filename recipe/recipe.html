Angelo's Chocolate

Ingredients

  • 3 cups any milk (or substitute) (or water)
  • 3 Tablespoons Dutch Process Cocoa Powder
  • 3 Tablespoons Sugar
  • Half a milk chocolate bar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of powdered cayenne pepper OR cinnamon.

Directions

  1. In a small saucepan, warm half a cup of milk / substitute on low.
  2. Separately mix together cocoa powder and sugar.
  3. Stir together milk and cocoa/sugar mixture in saucepan until cohesive. Then slowly add rest of milk while stirring.
  4. Add vanilla and cinnamon or cayenne pepper
  5. Chop up the chocolate bar, and add it in, stirring continuously.
  6. Once your hot cocoa is cohesive, add in other ingredients to taste. More cocoa for a more intense, bitter taste, more chocolate for added richness, and more sugar for added sweetness. I don't recommend adding more than Two Pinches of either spice as they can easily overpower the drink.
  7. Enjoy.
